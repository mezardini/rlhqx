FAQ

How does the lead generation process work?
Our process involves setting up targeted keywords related to your business. Our system then scans Reddit posts containing these keywords, identifies potential leads based on specific criteria, and delivers the leads to you.
Is the lead generation service customizable?
Yes, our service is customizable to meet your specific needs. You can provide us with a list of keywords relevant to your business, and we can adjust the criteria used to identify leads. Additionally, you can adjust the frequency and volume of leads delivered to you.
How does the credit system work?
The credit system allows you to manage your usage of the service based on your needs. 1 post suggested equals 1 credit - if you generate 100 posts, that is 100 credits. If you run out of credits, you can purchase additional credits in bundles to continue using the service.
